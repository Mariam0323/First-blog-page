Hello World! The beauty of nature is all around us and taking walks in nature is great for our physical and mental health.

I am a software developer with an interest in landscape photography. I hope to encourage people to enjoy the beauty of walks in nature and share their photos from their walks with the world.

Sir David Attenborough explains the mental benefits of the natural world and how it helped him in the following video Mental health and nature
You can get the natural world into your life, if you want to. Even in the most urban of circumstances.




My three favourite places for nature walks are:
  • Parks
  • Beach
  • Lake
